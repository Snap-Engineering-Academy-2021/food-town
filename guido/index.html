Instructions

1 -- Finely dice all the peppers, onions, and parsley.
2 -- Place the saffron in an aluminum pouch and place it on a medium flame for about 10 seconds each side. This helps bring out the flavor.
3 -- Bring the pan to heat.
4 -- Add olive oil, preferably Spanish Olive Oil.
5 -- Add peppers, onions, and garlic. And caramelize them.
6 -- Cut the spanish chorizo, add it to the pan.
7 -- Cut the chicken to desired size and add it to the pan.
8 -- Cook until all the juices from the chorizo fuse with the rest of the ingredients in the pan.
9 -- Add salt and pepper to taste.
10 -- Add the Bomba rice and the chicken broth.
11 -- Give it a light mix, then add the saffron.
12 -- Cook everything until the rice is al-dente.
13 -- Check the salt and add to taste.
14 -- Add the prawns and cook for about 3 - 5 min until they are almost done.
15 -- Add the Mussels and clams as the final ingredient, and let cook until done. About 5 minutes.
16 -- Serve and enjoy with some delicious cold Sangria!!!!
