Pesto Pasta with chicken from scratch

    • Making Pasta
    • Making Pesto Sauce
    • Making Chicken
    • Putting Everything Together






First lets star with the tool that will be used.

  • pasta maker
  • mesuring cups
  • blender
  • bowls
  • mixer or processor
  • knives









    • Ingredients to make the fettoccine pasta

    • This makes about one pound of dough (serves up to 4 - 6 people)
    • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons (170g) tipo 00flour, or 1 1/4 cups plus 2 tablespoons(170g) all-purpose flour plus some for dusting
    • 7 table spoons (55g) durum flour
    • 9 egg yolks
    • 1 tablespoon(15 ml) extra-virgin olive oil
    • 3 tablespoons (45 ml) water, plus more as needed

    Lets start making the pasta

    • In a work surface combine both flours and mixed, then after make a well in the center. Adding one ingredient at a time, add the eggs, yolks, water, and oil
    • Mix all ingredients till dough starts to build up (2-3 minutes) and if necessary add water about (15 ml) for the dough to come all together.
    • Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead it until it feels silky and smooth
    • Shape dough into a ball then flatten the ball into a disk. Cover dough and set aside for at least 30 minutes or you could wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerated for up to 3 days
    • To roll out the doug, cut it into 4 equal pieces and let the pieces sit at a room temperature for 10 minutes.The dough shouuld be cool but not cold. Shape each piece into an oval shape big enough to fit in your pasta roller
    • lightly flour your work surface and set your roller pasta to the widest setting. Lightly flour 1 piece of dough, pass it through the roller, and then lightly dust the rolled dought with flour, and brush off excess flour
    • Pass again on roller. Fold dough length wise and cut about 1 1/6 inch(6 mm) off of both corners and fold.
    • Repeat folding roller process for about once or twiece. After cut pasta of your choice.
    • After finishing cutting the pasta of your choice cook for four minutes in heated water and add salt of your choice (pinch)

    Ingridienst to make the pesto sauce

    • Olive oil (1 pt. - 480 ml)
    • Pine nuts (3 oz. - 90 g)
    • Fresh basil leaves (8 oz. - 240 g)
    • Garlic, chopped (1 Tbsp. - 15 ml)
    • Parmesan, grated (4 oz. - 120 g)
    • Romano, gratted (4 oz. - 120 g)
    • Salt and Pepper (TT - TT)

    Lets make pestp sauce

    • Place one third of the oil to the food blender or processor and all remaining ingredients
    • Blend or process until smooth. Add the remaing oil and blend a few seconds to incorporate.
    • You finished making the pesto sauce.

    Ingridienst to make the chicken

    • 1-2 Boneless chiken Breast
    • Salt and peper
    • 1/4 Unsalted Butter

    Lets make the chiken

    • Cut chicken breast in butterfly way. Then heat a pan with the unsalted butter.
    • Once butter is melted add chicken, as the chicken is cooking in one side add salt to the other side with a pinch of pepper, repeat for other side. (coo chicken about 9 to 10 minutes for each side.)

    Now lets put everything together

    • In a pan put your choice of pesto sauce, (reccomended 1 - 4 tablespoon), heat up to 30 - 45 seconds, then 1 cup of your fresh made pasta.
    • Mixed sauce and pasta. Then add chicken and heat up to 30- 45 seconds. Finaly serve and EAT!!!
