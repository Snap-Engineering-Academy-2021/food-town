Buko Pandan

Buko pandan is a Filipino fruit salad desert. It contains fresh young coconut (buko) and pandan flavoring. Pandan can be best described as a grassy vanilla with a hint of coconut flavor. It is served cold and popular during parties or fiestas.

Hello

, let's start cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup young coconut strips
  • 5 ounces condensed milk
  • 8 ounces Table Cream or all-purpose cream
  • Buko Pandan extract/flavoring
  • 1/2 cup sago pearls (tapioca pearls)
  • 1 can green gulaman (gelatin)
  • 1 bottle nata de coco (coconut gel)

Directions

  1. In a pot boil water and add the sago pears. Cook until translucent. Stir occasionally to prevent from sticking together. Turn off heat, drain excess water, rinse. Set aside to cool.
  2. Cut the gulaman into cubes.
  3. Drain out excess liquid from nata de coco and young coconut.
  4. In a large bowl add the young coconut, gulaman, condensed milk, table cream, sago pearls, nata de coco and mix.
  5. Add 3 drops of Buko Pandan flavoring and mix. You may add more to taste.
  6. Cool in fridge for at least 2 hours.
