Nachos

Recipe

For this recipe we will be making Nachos! We will begin by making our nacho cheese. Then we will be pouring it over our tray of nachos. We will then be adding the toppings we desire. I will first give the recipe for the nacho cheese then the toppings that I usually like to add on top.




Nacho Cheese Ingredients (by ChewOutLoud.com)

  • 8oz block of sharp cheese
  • 12oz evaporated milk
  • 1TB corn starch
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1/2tsp cumin
  • 1/4tsp cayenne (Can add more or less depending on spice level wanted)

Instructions

  • Simply toss the cheese with the spices above and the evaporated milk into a saucepan.
  • Cook, sit, and watch as it becomes a yummy and gooey nacho cheese.
  • Once gooey set aside until it's time for assembly.

Notes

  • Make sure to stir thoroughly as you cook all the ingredients so the cheese doesn't burn
  • As it cools it will become more thick, but it is really easy to heat up
  • Use a block of good quality sharp cheese as preshredded cheese may affect your results
  • I always save my leftovers in the fridge, and when I want more I just reheat it from a pot/pan

Toppings Ingredients

  • 2-3 Tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 Red Onion (diced)
  • 1 Romaine Lettuce (chopped)
  • 2 Cans of corn
  • Limes (wedges)
  • (Optional) Jalapenos (diced)
  • Choice of meat (Steak/1lb Ground Beef) [I have even used pulled pork!]

Assembly Instructions

  • Lay a sheet of aluminium foil on a baking tray.
  • Fill tray with tortilla chips, spread them out evenly.
  • Pour nacho cheese evenly all over the chips.
  • Sprinkle meat, tomatoes, onions, corn, lettuce, and jalapenos.
  • Finish it off by squeezing some lime over it.
