    Directions:

    1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions; drain well.
    2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat.
    3. Add mushrooms and shallots, and cook, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are tender and browned, about 5-7 minutes; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
    4. Stir in garlic and thyme until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    5. Whisk in flour until lightly browned, about 1 minute
    6. Gradually whisk in beef stock and Dijon. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until reduced and slightly thickened, about 4-5 minutes.
    7. Stir in pasta and sour cream until heated through, about 1-2 minutes.
    8. Stir in Parmesan until melted, about 1 minute.
    9. Stir in parsley; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve immediately.
