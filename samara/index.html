Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Total Time: 40 minutes

id="ingredients"

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Chicken Breast
  • 3/4 lbs fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb white mushrooms thickly sliced
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 1/2 cups half and half
  • 1/4 cup parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp sea salt or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper or to taste
  • 3 Tbsp olive olive
  • 1 Tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccini in a pot of salted water (4 qts water, 1 Tbsp salt,)
    according to package instructions then drain and set aside.

  2. Slice chicken into strips and season all over with salt and pepper.
    In a large skillet, heat 2 Tbsp olive oil over medium/high heat and
    saut√© chicken until lightly golden and cooked through (5 min).
    Remove chicken from the pan and cover to keep warm.

  3. Add half-n-half and simmer over medium/high heat 8-10 min,
    or until beginning to thicken. Add chicken back to the pan,
    add 1/4 cup parsley and season sauce
    to taste (1/2 to 1 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper).

  4. Add cooked pasta and stir to combine. Heat another minute until
    warmed through then turn off the heat, cover and let rest
    10-15 minutes then stir and serve garnished with parsley.
