Seaside Donuts Bakery

Must Try Picks!

Ham & Cheese Croissant with Jalapenos

The Ham & Cheese Croissant with Jalapenos is an AMAZING peace of work from heaven. The kind workers warm it up for you on the spot, so the cheese is melty and the bread is super duper soft! For an unforgettable experience in your mouth, this is the dish to get when check this place out!

Matcha Thai Tea

The Matcha Thai Tea is like nothing else, combining two of the world's most amazing things, matcha and thai tea. Sweet and smooth, you don't want to forget ordering this on your next trip!

Cronut

The Cronut is a classic at Seaside Bakery. Imagine a perfectly glazed donut that's sweet and light. Now imagine a fliffy, buttery croissant. Together, they're an unforgettable combination you're sure to come back for!
