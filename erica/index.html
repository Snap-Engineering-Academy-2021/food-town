California Pizza Kitchen

The first restaurant was opened in Beverly Hills in 1985. After the first restaurant opened, it quickly expanded to other parts of the world. This includes over 30 states in the US and 11 countries. Not only can you find California Pizza restaurants in many places, but you can also find some of their famous pizzas at some grocery stores in the frozen ailse. This way, you can eat their famous pizzas while you are out and about, or in the comfort of your own home!

Almost all their dishes are good, but there are two in particular that are quite tasty.

If you decide that the Butter Cake is not for you, I recommend trying the Salted Caramel Pudding. This dessert is the perfect mixture of sweet and salty, which is bound to make anyone smile!
